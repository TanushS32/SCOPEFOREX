About Us

A trusted RBI-licensed financial partner committed to serving your foreign exchange and financial service needs with integrity and precision.

Who We Are

Established with a commitment to excellence, Scope Forex Pvt. Ltd. is a fully RBI-licensed entity providing a comprehensive range of forex and financial services. Our offerings span foreign currency exchange, forex cards, outward remittances, travel insurance, and international money receipt solutions.

We take pride in being a customer-first company — combining fast, secure transactions with a highly professional approach. With partnerships across globally recognized platforms like Western Union, MoneyGram, RIA, and Indian giants like RBL Bank and Thomas Cook, we ensure trustworthy and seamless service experiences.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses with transparent, reliable, and compliant foreign exchange and financial solutions through innovation, trust, and unmatched service delivery.

Our Vision

To become one of India’s most respected and customer-centric financial service providers, setting new benchmarks in operational excellence and client satisfaction.

Licensed & Regulated

Scope Forex Pvt. Ltd. is fully licensed by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) to offer money changing and remittance services.

We adhere strictly to RBI guidelines, KYC norms, and compliance protocols to ensure every transaction is secure, traceable, and regulatory approved.
